About Echosight

Echosight was founded in 2025 to empower ecology consultancies with tailored support that bridges ecological expertise and business efficiency. We help organisations adapt, modernise, and thrive in an industry facing rapid environmental and regulatory change.

Our services are built to:

  • Deliver specialist support for protected species, including bats, great crested newts, and reptiles
  • Streamline data analysis, reporting, and acoustic workflows with precision
  • Scale flexibly to match seasonal demand and staffing gaps
  • Modernise operations through smart tools, CRM systems, and agile strategies
  • Maximise sustainability impact through long-term, values-driven guidance

At Echosight, we combine practical know-how with deep ecological insight—so you can focus on what matters most: delivering high-quality environmental work, efficiently and ethically.

Services

Providing expert ecological and data-driven solutions to empower businesses and consultancies. We help you optimise resources, streamline operations, and enhance sustainability through tailored support and strategic expertise.

Ecological Surveying

Targeted surveys for bats, great crested newts, reptiles, and protected habitats—delivering precise data to meet conservation goals and regulatory demands.

GIS & Spatial Analysis

Spatial intelligence for habitat mapping, impact assessments, and mitigation planning—using R, QGIS, ArcGIS, MAGIC and tailored data workflows to inform decisions.

Acoustic Data Analysis

Bioacoustic surveys and analysis using AI-enhanced tools—monitoring bat activity, bird calls, and ecological soundscapes with scalable data solutions.

Project & Operations

Project delivery, team collaboration, and system integration—bringing CRM, DevOps, and workflow design to life for busy ecological teams.
