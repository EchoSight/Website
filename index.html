About Echosight

Echosight was founded in 2025 to help ecology consultancies do more—with less stress. We bridge ecological expertise and operational smarts, supporting teams through complex projects, tight deadlines, and shifting regulations.

We provide:

  • Specialist species support for bats, great crested newts, reptiles, and protected habitats
  • Streamlined workflows for data analysis, reporting, and acoustic interpretation
  • Flexible resourcing to meet seasonal demand and cover staffing gaps
  • Operational upgrades from CRMs to custom workflows, so your systems work for you
  • Strategic sustainability built on real-world insight and long-term thinking

At Echosight, we combine practical know-how with deep ecological insight—so you can focus on what matters most: delivering high-quality environmental work, efficiently and ethically.

Specialist support. Smarter systems. Real results.

Services
